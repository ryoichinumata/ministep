About the service

What is MiniStep?

MiniStep is a habit-building web app for people who want to keep going but struggle to stay consistent.
Instead of big goals, it delivers one tiny daily challenge through a gacha-style draw.

What you can do with MiniStep

  • Draw one small challenge per day
  • Pick a category that matches your current mood (self-care, focus, social, and more)
  • Log each completed day and build a streak of small wins

MiniStep isn't just for your best days — it's designed to be there
on tired days and low-motivation days too.

Who is MiniStep for?

  • People who keep giving up after just a few days
  • Perfectionists who burn out before they even get started
  • Anyone who wants to act but doesn't know where to begin
  • People looking to gently build self-confidence over time
  • Anyone who wants to tidy up their daily life — study, sleep, focus, or just feel better

Design principles

  1. Lower the bar as far as it goes
    Every challenge is small enough that you can think: "Even I could do this."
  2. No self-blame
    We care more about "I took one step today" than counting consecutive days.
  3. Match your current energy
    Whether you're feeling great or running on empty, MiniStep has a tiny step that fits.

How to use MiniStep

  1. Open https://ministep.net in your browser
  2. Pick a mood category and tap the draw button
  3. Try the tiny challenge that appears — do what you can
  4. Tap "Mark done" to record today's step

MiniStep is free and works without sign-up.
Features and content will keep improving over time.
